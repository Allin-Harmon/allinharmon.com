    Web Design

    Though new to web design, I have learned much about it in the last year. Thanks to the help of my web design instructors I am proficient in HTML5 and CSS3.     Print Design

    I worked at a print shop for two years doing everything from typesetting to running a printing press and before that I was a desktop publisher for a repro shop for seven years. I feel this gives me a clear understanding of the needs and demands of good print design.

    Advertising

They gave me a great insight into the philosophy of design with the good of the business being the most important part.
