I served in the US Army on active duty from June 2006 to November 2009. After my time on active duty, I was in the Army Reserves. I received an Honorable discharge in May 2014. Serving my country helped me become more disciplined and better able to complete difficult tasks.

In addition to my love for coding, I also love graphic design, photo manipulation, building computers and video games. They all feed into my love for puzzle solving. I love to solve problems. The more difficult the problem, the greater the feeling of euphoria when I solve it.
