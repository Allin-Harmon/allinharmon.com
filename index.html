My Experience

I have and solid understanding of HTML and CSS. I am learning to master JavaScript. I am proficient in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator and In Design. I have been a Front-End Web Developer, a Graphic Designer, and a Soldier among other things. Take a look at my resume to find out more.

My Education

I graduated from the Art Institute of Atlanta, Summa cum Laude, with a Batchelor of Arts in Advertising in 2012. After graduating, I used my remaining credit from my GI Bill to take a lot of Web Design classes and fell in love. In 2020, I graduated from the Code Boot Camp at The Clubhou.se.

My Military Experience

I served in the US Army on active duty from June 2006 to November 2009. After my time on active duty, I was in the Army Reserves. I received an Honorable discharge in May 2014. Serving my country helped me become more disciplined and better able to complete difficult tasks.

My Passions

In addition to my love for coding, I also love graphic design, photo manipulation, building computers and video games. They all feed into my love for puzzle solving. I love to solve problems. The more difficult the problem, the greater the feeling of euphoria when I solve it.
